About Us

Who We Are

The Indigenous Cooperative Marketing and Trading Society Limited — known locally as Manples Kava Coop — is a community-owned cooperative dedicated to the sustainable production, marketing, and export of premium Ni-Vanuatu kava.

Founded on the principles of fair trade and collective empowerment, we bring together growers from across the islands to ensure they receive fair prices while delivering the highest quality kava to markets domestically and internationally.

Our mission is to strengthen rural livelihoods, preserve indigenous agricultural knowledge, and build a thriving cooperative economy for generations to come.
